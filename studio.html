BLUCINE / STUDIO / METHOD

STUDIO

Blucine works through structure, not accumulation. Each project is treated as a controlled relation between image, space, material and timing.

POSITION

A visual structure operating with a small number of clear fields and a precise editorial attitude.

FRAME

HOW THE WORK IS BUILT

Three principles organise the process.

01

CLARITY

The first task is always to reduce noise and identify what truly carries the project.

02

ATMOSPHERE

Projects must hold a readable mood. Light, pacing and surface are never secondary.

03

COHERENCE

Typography, space, imagery and interaction have to speak the same language.

FIELDS

EXHIBITION / INSTALLATION / DIRECTION / DIGITAL

A compact field structure keeps the identity strong and avoids dispersion.
