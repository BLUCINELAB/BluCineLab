SPATIAL OBJECT

A case-study placeholder for a large-format installation where presence, silhouette and spatial impact matter more than excess. The object is conceived as both form and image: compact, readable, immediate.

Role

Installation Concept

Scale, gesture, spatial emphasis and public readability.

Field

Temporary Structure

High-visibility installation designed to hold attention immediately.

Status

Protected Placeholder

The actual commission is translated here into an abstract editorial form.

Context

SCALE AS MESSAGE

The installation is imagined as one concentrated gesture. Its role is not simply to occupy space, but to organise how the space is perceived from distance, in movement and under pressure.

Method

SILHOUETTE BEFORE DETAIL

The logic begins with shape and proportion. Only later come material, rhythm and surface. The aim is to produce something memorable, but controlled enough to remain precise and elegant.
