Context

The Project

The installation is imagined as one compact, readable gesture. Its role is not simply to occupy space, but to organise how the space is perceived from distance, in movement and under pressure.

Approach

Method

The logic begins with silhouette and scale. Only later come material, rhythm and detail. The aim is to produce something direct enough to be memorable, but controlled enough to remain elegant.
