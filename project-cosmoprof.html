Context

THE PROJECT

The installation is imagined as one compact, readable gesture. Its role is not simply to occupy space, but to organise how the space is perceived from distance, in movement and under pressure.

Method

SILHOUETTE BEFORE DETAIL

The logic begins with shape and scale. Only later come material, rhythm and surface. The aim is to produce something direct enough to be memorable, but controlled enough to remain elegant.
