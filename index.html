About

Not an agency. A constructed visual structure.

Visual research, production discipline and perceptual construction.

BluCineLab works across cinema, installations, immersive environments and narrative systems.

Each project is approached as a built condition: concept, visual rhythm, atmosphere, technical execution and perceptual effect.

The goal is not to decorate space, but to organize image, sound and matter into a coherent experience.

Current activation

Cosmoprof 2026 / Cube system

Live installation built through reflection, projection, contrast and spatial perception.

A reflective golden cube placed within a Mediterranean scenic field, with internal projections of Marco Glaviano imagery and a layered sound structure unfolding between interior immersion and exterior perception.

Format Immersive installation
Spatial core Reflective golden cube
Environment Mediterranean scenography
Image material Marco Glaviano projections
Sound logic Internal / external layering
Perceptual key Reflection / distortion / contrast
