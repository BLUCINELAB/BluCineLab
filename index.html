Spatial image systems for exhibition, cinema and live environments.

BluCineLab develops visual structures where image, space, sound and perception are treated as one system. This temporary page presents the current installation developed for Cosmoprof 2026.

About

Not an agency. A constructed visual structure.

BluCineLab works across cinema, installations, immersive environments and narrative systems.

Each project is approached as a built condition: concept, visual rhythm, atmosphere, technical execution and perceptual effect.

The goal is not to decorate space, but to organize image, sound and matter into a coherent experience.

Current activation

Cosmoprof 2026 / Cube system

A golden cube placed within a Mediterranean scenic environment, with internal projections of Marco Glaviano’s imagery and a layered sound structure unfolding between interior immersion and exterior perception.

Spatial core Golden cube
Environment Mediterranean scenography
Image material Marco Glaviano projections
