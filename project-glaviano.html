CONFIDENTIAL / EXHIBITION / SPATIAL DESIGN

EXHIBITION PROJECT

A confidential exhibition project conceived as a spatial and atmospheric device, where rhythm, proportion and image placement guide the reading of the work.

ROLE

EXHIBITION STRUCTURE

Spatial organisation, pacing and overall atmosphere.

FIELD

CULTURAL DISPLAY

Image-to-space relation, visitor movement and visual hierarchy.

STATUS

CONFIDENTIAL

The project is presented in anonymised form during a protected phase.

CONTEXT

THE PROJECT

The intervention was designed to support the work through layout, silence and pacing, avoiding decorative excess and reinforcing the perceptive strength of the exhibition itself.

METHOD

THE APPROACH

The project focused on relation: image to wall, wall to room, room to movement. The result had to feel calm, legible and structurally coherent.

PROJECT SNAPSHOT

Type

Exhibition

Focus

Atmosphere, pacing, wall-image relation

Output

Spatial coherence and controlled visual rhythm
