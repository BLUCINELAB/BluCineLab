EXHIBITION / SPATIAL DESIGN

MARCO GLAVIANO

Exhibition project developed as a spatial and atmospheric device, with attention to rhythm, visual hierarchy and the relationship between image and environment.

ROLE

Exhibition design / spatial atmosphere / visual organisation

LOCATION

Palazzo Vassé

FIELD

Exhibition / spatial design / visual direction

CONTEXT

THE PROJECT

The intervention was conceived as an exhibition system able to support the work through layout, atmosphere and visual pacing rather than through decorative excess.

APPROACH

THE METHOD

The project focused on relation: image to wall, wall to room, room to movement. The space had to feel coherent, calm and precise.
